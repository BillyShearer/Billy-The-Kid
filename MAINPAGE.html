Billy The Kid






Billy The Kid is a highly energetic Dj/Entertainer that has travelled the globe performing at all kinds of venues, to all walks of life.

Specialising in bass heavy remixes of songs you love, Billy The Kid can cater to any demographic from 70s disco to the depths of psy trance.

Billy The Kid's main seller is his infectious energy, paired with his attitude that 'why would I expect anyone to dance to my songs if i don't?'

Some of the stages he has graced include the Whistler Snowboard & Ski Festival(s), Portugese Boat Parties, Mexican beach parties and anywhere through to Aussie pubs & clubs
